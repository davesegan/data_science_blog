--- title: "Mono Lake Water Levels" author: "David Segan" date: "10/20/2019" output: html_document ---

Visualization of Mono Lake water levels over time and the effect of diversions to meet the water demands of Los Angeles.

Data sources: 1850-1912 from Stine, Scott (based on occasional observations and San Francisco precipitation). 1912-1979 from LADWP and USGS compilations. 1979-present from Los Angeles Aqueduct Daily Reports, and observations by the Mono Lake Committee. Compiled by the Mono Lake Committee.
